About Me

I am a passionate product manager with a focus on building innovative and user-friendly financial products. With a strong background in both B2B and B2C solutions, I work to create seamless user journeys that drive customer satisfaction and business success.

Projects

Here are some of the major projects I’ve worked on:

UIDAI eKYC Integration

Integrated UIDAI’s eKYC API into the bank’s onboarding flow for real-time borrower authentication. Led API design, built secure ADV storage, and overcame regulatory and data mapping challenges.

Outcome:

Enabled the bank’s first vertical using UIDAI API, saving over ₹12L annually.

Decommissioning Legacy LOS

Spearheaded the redesign of a rigid LOS into a modular platform integrated with LMS, Collection, and API systems. Created data flows, screens, and a new BRE to handle complex loan logic.

Outcome:

Delivered a flexible, modern lending system—reduced tech debt and enabled faster launches.

Managing Government Business

Led internal systems to support government lending programs like NRLM, NULM, and SHG. Improved PSL tracking and SMF categorization.

Outcome:

Helped the bank stay compliant with RBI PSL mandates and reduce manual intervention.
